# Patch Notes

## Gameplay
- War stability cost now scales with duration. It starts lower: 0.1 with CB, 0.2 without (was 0.25 with CB, 0.5 without). But it increases each year (0.1 with CB, 0.2 without). This makes long wars more punishing
- Army morale recovery increased: now +10/month (was +1)
- New action: Appoint Bishop. It costs 60 Christianity influence, grants +1 Christianity Yearly for 10 years

## Bugfixes
- Research Cost modifier now applies: technologies and upgrades that modify research cost now actually affect tech costs
