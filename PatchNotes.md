# Patch Notes

## Gameplay
- War stability cost now scales with duration. It starts lower: 0.1 with CB, 0.2 without (was 0.25 with CB, 0.5 without). But it increases each year (0.1 with CB, 0.2 without). This makes long wars more punishing. War monthly consequences tooltip now shows a stability cost breakdown table.
- Army morale recovery increased: now +10/month (was +1).
- New action: Appoint Bishop. It costs 60 Christianity influence, grants +1 Christianity Yearly for 10 years.
- Top panel resource tooltips (Administrative, Diplomatic, Military) now show detailed breakdowns with monthly income indicators.
- Internal Affairs page redesigned as a side panel with improved layout.

## Bugfixes
- Fix a bug where technologies and upgrades that modify research cost do not have effect.
